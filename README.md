# Minecraft Origins RPG

## Overview

Welcome to the Minecraft Origins RPG project! This datapack and resourcepack add classic RPG character classes to Minecraft using the [Origins](https://origins.readthedocs.io/) mod. Choose your class and gain unique abilities, playstyles, and progression paths that bring tabletop RPG depth to your Minecraft adventures.

## RPG Classes

### Alchemist
*Master of potions and brews*
- Negative potion effects do not affect them (negated or replaced with positive effects)
- Brewed potions last longer or are stronger (with access to extra recipes for enhanced potions)
- Can use special abilities only when debuffed, turning weaknesses into strengths

### Armorer
*The armor specialist and defensive guardian*
- Different armor sets provide different buffs and protection bonuses
- Armor repairs at reduced cost
- Each armor tier grants unique benefits:
  - **Leather** - Increased movement speed (on land, air, or sea)
  - **Iron** - High knockback resistance (100% or near-total)
  - **Gold** - +1 Looting level and Resistance II
  - **Diamond** - +1 Fortune level
  - **Netherite** - Fire Resistance and Piglin Friends status

### Bard
*The enchanting musician and party supporter*
- Play musical notes using arrow keys or key combinations with WASD
- Each song provides powerful effects to the Bard and nearby allies:
  - **Hero of the Village** - Grants Hero of the Village status to all nearby players
  - **Eurobeat Song** - Grants Speed II to the Bard for 3 minutes
  - **Song of Storms** - Changes weather (long cooldown)
  - **Potion Rain** - Drops random lingering potions (3 per use)
  - **Steve's Lava Chicken** - Spawns random animals or neutral mobs on fire (villager, turtle, frog, panda, fox, goat, pufferfish, etc.)

### Chef
*The culinary spellcaster and food enchanter*
- Cooked food grants special buffs, primarily Regeneration
- Gold Carrots grant 2 Absorption hearts
- Pumpkin Pie and Rabbit Stew provide extended Saturation
- Placeable food items cast special spells:
  - **Chef's Cake** - Grants Creative Flight for 2 minutes and Slow Fall for 3 minutes to those who eat it
  - **Pumpkin Placement** - Removes the pumpkin and spawns an Icing Golem (Snow Golem)
  - **Hay Bale Placement** - Special effect when placed
  - **Honey Block Healing** - Restores 1 health per tick while inside
- Bone Meal has a 50% chance to not be consumed when used

### Dragoon
*The aerial warrior with mounted combat prowess*
- Built-in elytra wings but for slower, more controlled gliding
- Petting animals reduces growth time by half (or reduces down to 2 minutes) with a 5-minute base cooldown
- Access to special saddle recipes for mounted combat (monkey power)

### Thief
*The nimble rogue and poison master*
- Crafts 16 arrows per recipe instead of 4 (enhanced arrow production) (elf)
- Increased speed in darkness and at night (rat)
- Wall climbing ability when unarmored or wearing leather armor (toggle on/off)
- Poison attacks inflict powerful effects:
  - Poison Potato - Poison IV lasting 5 seconds
  - Spider Eye - Poison II lasting 10 seconds
  - Can eat these items anytime, even when full
- Complete immunity to poison damage
- Fortune VII (luck level 7)

### Werewolf Subclass *(Optional)*
*The primal full moon transformer*
- Full moon grants massive combat buffs
- Automatic or optional transformation (partial? big?)
- Available only after completing the werewolf questline and obtaining permission from the admin (Yeti)
- Potential for custom player model transformations

## Conclusion

This Origins RPG project transforms Minecraft into a more immersive role-playing experience by giving players distinct class identities with meaningful abilities and playstyles. Whether you prefer the supportive melodies of the Bard, the nimble shadows of the Thief, or the culinary magic of the Chef, each class offers unique challenges and rewards. Dive in and discover which class matches your playstyle—or try them all!

## Developer Instructions
*Modpack Setup*
- Download and install Modrinth launcher
- Use custom modpack 'Origins Fantasy Classes 1.0.0' to create a new mincraft game profile
- Start the modrinth game launcher on the new profile

*Datapack Installation*
- Navigate to your local github project folder
- In the minecraft game launcher
  - Go to 'Singleplayer'
  - Select 'Create New World'
  - Navigate to 'More' => 'Data Packs'
  - On the left hand side under 'Data Packs' drag and drop (unzipped) folders for
    - sneakyclassdata
    - sneakyclassresource
- Start the world and enjoy the new classes!
